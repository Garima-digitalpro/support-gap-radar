# Time-off policy

Employees request annual or sick leave in the HR portal. Select a full day or half day, add the dates, and submit the request to the manager.

Up to five unused annual leave days carry into the next year and expire on March 31. Public holidays during approved annual leave are not deducted. A medical certificate is required from the third consecutive sick day.

The policy does not explain probation, emergency leave, negative balances, or cancellation cutoffs.
