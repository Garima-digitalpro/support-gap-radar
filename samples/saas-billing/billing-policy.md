# Subscription billing and cancellation

## Cancel a subscription

Only the workspace owner can cancel a paid subscription.

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Select **Billing**.
3. Choose **Manage subscription**.
4. Select **Cancel plan** and confirm.

The workspace remains active until the end of the current billing period. Before that date, the owner can return to the Billing page and reactivate the subscription.

After the paid period ends, the workspace becomes read-only for 30 days while the owner decides whether to export or delete its data.

## Billing profile and invoices

Workspace owners can update the legal name, address, and VAT ID under **Billing profile**. Updated details appear on future invoices and on the latest open invoice. Finalized historical invoices cannot be edited; contact support to request a correction note.

Invoices are available under **Billing > Invoices**. Owners can download one PDF or use **Download all CSV** for an annual record.

The billing currency can be changed only for the next billing period. A finalized invoice cannot be reissued in another currency.

> This article does not define refund eligibility, refund timing, renewal exceptions, downgrade credits, or failed-card handling.
